Welcome to the world of Monster
This is a modern port of University at Buffalo's Monster, itself a long-lost descendent of VMS Monster, an early MUD written by Rich Skrenta.
  • Play Monster in your browser.
    • Note that this is a work in progress, and the game will be periodically wiped as we work towards a finished version. You will need to recreate your account post-wipe.
  • Report any bugs or issues on the MonsterMUD Forum.
  • Find out more about Evennia, the MUD engine powering this new version of Monster.
  • Read the backstory of the effort to resurrect a 30-year-old VAX Pascal game.
